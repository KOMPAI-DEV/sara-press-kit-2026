Biography

Sara Callie grew up singing in Danish gospel choirs, absorbing from an early age the belief that singing is about emotional delivery, not just hitting notes — and that voices are best thought of in layers rather than single melodies. In 2022 she competed in the Danish Melodi Grand Prix with the group Confessions and the song “Hallelujah”, finishing second on the national stage. That experience, combined with a place at the Martin Garrix Music Academy, gave her the confidence and drive to pursue a solo career. She relocated to Stockholm and built her team with the indie music company Komp Music, who signed her in early 2026 to launch her solo career. She writes and arranges all her own vocals alongside a live studio choir — a process she calls building a sonic cathedral. Her debut single “Ruins of Us”, released 12 June 2026, is a song about resilience and the belief that rebuilding yourself after a heartbreak can become your greatest strength. Her follow-up “Godspeed” is coming soon. Many songs are writen and produced and will be relesed dusinf the 2026 and the worls will see the full potential of Sara Callie. Debut EP is planed due in the end of 2026.
